About Me

Arda was born and raised in Izmir, Turkey and is currently based in Lethbridge, AB.

He aims to graduate from Lethbridge College and further his education with a bachelor's degree, which would equip him with a strong portfolio, increase his chances of finding an internship, and ultimately prepare him for the Canadian workplace experience.
