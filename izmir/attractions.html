Attractions of Izmir

Izmir was once called Smyrna, which is the name that still remains in use in other languages like Greek or Spanish. As of today, Izmir is considered as the most Westernized city in Turkey.

Attractions of Izmir

The center of Izmir is especially popular among tourists. It is packed with people, street food vendors can be seen on every corner, with smells and flavors coming from all sides.
