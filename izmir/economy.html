Economy of Izmir

Izmir's economy largely relies on its seaport, which is a major hub for international trade and export and contributes to one-fifth of the total exports in Turkey.

Economy of Izmir

Izmir serves as a popular work spot for services, logistics, and agriculture industries. Izmir is also a start-up and business-friendly city as it attracts investments from many Turkish and international firms. Thanks to its warm climate, it is a leading center of production and processing of products such as olives, grapes, cotton, and tobacco.
