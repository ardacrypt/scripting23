People of Izmir

People of Izmir can be considered as the most liberal, open-minded, most secular, most progressive people of the country.

People of Izmir

Although the majority part of the population is Muslim, mosques are not too noticeable, women don't cover their heads with veils and alcohol can be easily found at restaurants and shops. When it comes to hospitality, Turkish people take the lead. In a city known for its hot-blooded people, you may easily find yourself invited to a dinner or lunch at one's home. Tourists are highly valued, and it is considered an honour for them to invite and take care of you. And if you are invited to a home, make sure to take off your shoes first just like any other Turkish does. This way, outdoor dust and dirt will be prevented from getting inside.
